<p style="font-size:14px;max-width:600px;text-align:justify">Our Visualization Group at NYU Tandon School of Engineering conducts cutting-edge research that spans the fields of data visualization, explainable and steerable aritfical intelligence (AI), and human-computer interaction. Our research directions include novel rendering and display of large and complex imaging and high-dimensional feature data, interactive analysis interfaces, and evaluation with domain practitioners, currently with a focus on the biomedical field.</p>
