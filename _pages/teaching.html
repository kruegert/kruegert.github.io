This included project management, task assignments, weekly meetings, and personal management.

Since 2021 I am a subgroup leader at the Visual Computing Group (VCG), Harvard University for collaborating on biomedical data analysis and visualization with the Laboratory of Systems Pharmacology, Harrvard Medical School.
